#### banana chocolate cookies

* 2x bananas
* 200 grams of desiccated coconut
* mash them up together
* put them in the oven
   * bake them for up 100-200 degree celsius
   * for 10 minutes
* melt some dark chocolate (100 grams)
   * put it over the done cookie 
* cool them down in the fridge.

* enjoy
