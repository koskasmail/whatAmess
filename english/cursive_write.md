## 🌐 Free Online Resources for Learning English Cursive

### **1. YouTube Lessons (Free & Visual)**
Great for beginners because you can watch the hand movements.
- Search for: **“Cursive writing for beginners”**
- Channels like *Lets Learn*, *Cursive Writing*, and *Calligraphy Masters* have step‑by‑step videos.

### **2. Free Printable Worksheets**
You can print them or practice on a tablet.
- Search for: **“Cursive handwriting worksheets PDF free”**
- Look for worksheets that include:
  - Individual letters (a–z)
  - Joining letters
  - Words and sentences

### **3. Online Practice Websites**
These sites let you practice or download sheets:
- **HandwritingWorksheets.com** – create your own cursive practice sheets  
- **K5Learning.com** – free cursive worksheets  
- **Education.com** – free samples (you can download a few without paying)

## ✍️ A Simple Daily Practice Plan (10–15 minutes)

### **Day 1–3: Learn the basic strokes**
- Loops  
- Curves  
- Upstrokes and downstrokes  

### **Day 4–10: Practice lowercase letters**
Start with easy ones:  
`a, c, e, i, l, u`  
Then move to:  
`m, n, r, s, t`  
Finally:  
`b, f, g, h, j, k, p, q, y, z`

### **Day 11–15: Practice uppercase letters**

### **Day 16+: Practice joining letters**
Examples:  
`la, le, li, ca, ce, co, th, wh, st`

### **After 2–3 weeks: Write full sentences**
Try copying:
- Short quotes  
- Song lyrics  
- Your daily notes
